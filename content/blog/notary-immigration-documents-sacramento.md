### California Requirements for Notarizing Immigration Forms

California law mandates specific procedures for all notarizations, including those for immigration documents. According to California Government Code Section 8205, a notary public must verify the identity of the signer using satisfactory evidence, such as a state-issued driver's license or passport. The signer must be physically present before the notary, and the notary must complete an entry in their official journal for each notarization performed.
