## Sacramento County Marriage License Requirements

Before any ceremony can happen, you need a marriage license. This is a California state requirement.

Here is the quick version. Both parties must appear in person at the Sacramento County Clerk Recorder office at 600 8th Street, Sacramento, CA 95814. Bring valid government issued photo ID and your Social Security numbers. The fee is $90 for a public license or $100 for a confidential license.

California has no waiting period. You can get your license and have your ceremony the same day. The license is valid for 90 days. For a full breakdown of the process, read the complete [wedding license guide for Sacramento, CA](/blog/wedding-license-sacramento-ca-guide/).
