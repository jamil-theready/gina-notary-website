## What is an Apostille and Why Do I Need One for Colombia?

An apostille is a special certificate. It authenticates the seals and signatures of officials on public documents. This authentication is required when documents are to be used in foreign countries that are members of The Hague Apostille Convention. Colombia is one such country. This means a California document intended for use in Colombia needs an apostille. Without it, your document might not be accepted by Colombian authorities.
