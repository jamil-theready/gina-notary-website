## DMV Document Notarization in Sacramento

Many DMV transactions require notarized documents. Vehicle title transfers, powers of attorney, lien sales, and record corrections all need a notary's seal to be accepted by the California Department of Motor Vehicles.

## DMV Documents We Notarize

### Vehicle Title Transfers (REG 227)

When selling or gifting a vehicle, the title transfer often requires notarized signatures, especially when one party cannot visit the DMV in person. Gina notarizes the title and any supporting documents so the transfer processes smoothly.

### Power of Attorney for Vehicle Transactions (REG 260)

The REG 260 form allows someone to handle DMV transactions on your behalf. This is commonly used when you are out of state, deployed military, or otherwise unable to visit the DMV. The form requires notarization before the DMV will accept it.

### Duplicate Title Applications

If your vehicle title is lost, damaged, or stolen, applying for a duplicate requires a notarized application. Gina can notarize your application at your home or office.

### Lien Sale Documents

Auto repair shops, tow companies, and storage facilities use the lien sale process to recover unpaid charges. The lien sale paperwork includes multiple documents that require notarization. Gina is experienced with lien sale document packages.

### Bill of Sale and Odometer Disclosures

Private vehicle sales often include a notarized bill of sale and federal odometer disclosure statement. These documents protect both the buyer and seller by creating an official record of the transaction.

### Affidavits and Declarations

The DMV sometimes requires notarized affidavits to correct errors on vehicle records, confirm vehicle identification numbers, or verify ownership history. Gina notarizes these declarations throughout Sacramento.
