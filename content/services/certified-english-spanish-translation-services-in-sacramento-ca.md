When official documents need to cross language barriers, accuracy is everything. A single mistranslation can delay an immigration case, void a legal filing, or create problems with a government agency.

### Immigration Documents

USCIS requires certified English translations for all foreign language documents submitted with immigration petitions. Birth certificates, marriage certificates, police clearances, and other documents from Spanish speaking countries need accurate, certified translations. Gina's translations meet USCIS standards and include the required certification statement.

Court filings, contracts, affidavits, powers of attorney, and other legal documents require precise translation. ### Business Documents
