## Certified Loan Signing Agent in Sacramento

When you are buying, refinancing, or selling a home, the loan signing is where it all comes together. Dozens of legal documents need to be signed, initialed, and notarized correctly. One mistake can delay your closing or cause your loan to fall through.

Gina Gonzalez is a certified loan signing agent serving Sacramento and the greater Sacramento metro area. She is NNA certified with over 6,000 documents notarized and a background as a former lawyer. ## Loan Signing Services

### Purchase Transactions

Buying a home involves signing the deed of trust, promissory note, closing disclosures, and many supporting documents. Gina reviews the package in advance and walks you through each document at your kitchen table, office, or wherever is most convenient.

### Refinance Signings

Refinancing your mortgage can save thousands over the life of your loan. The signing process is typically faster than a purchase. Gina ensures all documents are signed correctly so your new loan funds on schedule.

### Seller Signings

Sellers have their own set of documents to sign at closing. Grant deeds, transfer disclosures, and escrow instructions all require notarization. Gina handles seller signings at your location throughout Sacramento.

### Reverse Mortgage Signings

Reverse mortgages have specific requirements and additional disclosures. Gina is experienced with reverse mortgage document packages and takes extra time to ensure borrowers understand the terms. Required counseling documentation must be completed before the signing.

### Home Equity and HELOC Signings

Home equity loans and lines of credit require their own set of notarized documents. Gina handles these signings with the same thoroughness as a full purchase or refinance transaction.
