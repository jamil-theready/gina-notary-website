## Loan Signing Services

### Purchase Transactions

Buying a home involves signing the deed of trust, promissory note, closing disclosures, and many supporting documents. Gina reviews the package in advance and walks you through each document at your kitchen table, office, or wherever is most convenient.

### Refinance Signings

Refinancing your mortgage can save thousands over the life of your loan. The signing process is typically faster than a purchase. Gina ensures all documents are signed correctly so your new loan funds on schedule.

### Seller Signings

Sellers also have documents to sign at closing. Grant deeds, transfer disclosures, and escrow instructions all require notarization. Gina handles seller signings at your location throughout the Sacramento metro area.

### Reverse Mortgage Signings

Reverse mortgages have unique requirements and additional disclosures. Gina has experience with reverse mortgage packages and takes the time to explain each document clearly. For Spanish speaking seniors, having a bilingual signing agent removes a significant barrier.
