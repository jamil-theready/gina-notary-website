Contact Gina at (415) 948-9967. Let her know what documents need notarization, how many signers are involved, and your preferred location and time. She will provide a quote and confirm your appointment.

### 2. Prepare Your Documents and ID

She arrives at your chosen location with all necessary supplies. She verifies your identity, reviews the documents, witnesses your signatures, and applies her notary seal. The entire process typically takes 15 to 30 minutes for standard documents.
