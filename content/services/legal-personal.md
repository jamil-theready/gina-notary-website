## Documents We Notarize

### Power of Attorney (POA)

A Power of Attorney gives someone you trust the legal authority to act on your behalf. Whether you need a **General POA**, **Durable POA**, or **Limited POA**, Gina ensures the document meets California requirements and is properly executed.

Common scenarios include adult children obtaining POA for aging parents, business owners delegating authority during travel, and families planning for medical decisions.

### Healthcare Directives

An **Advance Healthcare Directive** lets you specify your medical treatment preferences and appoint someone to make healthcare decisions if you cannot communicate them yourself. California law requires notarization for these documents.

### Affidavits and Sworn Statements

Affidavits are sworn statements made under oath and notarized for use in legal proceedings, immigration matters, or official business. Gina verifies your identity and confirms the statement was made willingly.

### Name Changes and Corrections

Legal name changes require notarized documents for court filing. Whether you are changing your name after marriage, divorce, or for personal reasons, Gina handles the notarization process.

### Prenuptial Agreements

Prenuptial agreements protect both parties before marriage. While the agreement should be drafted by an attorney, Gina provides the notarization to make it legally binding.
